About Us

Quality Notes is a (beautifully punned) music rating platform where anyone and everyone can rank the latest songs. Think Yelp for music. Or Uber for Billboard. Or Doordash for Pitchfork. Right now we're sorting through a bunch of one-line summaries, but if Andreessen ever gets back to me, we'll finally have something set.

We were born out of a dorm room, out of the realization that although there had numerous attempts to democratize and simplify music rating, there was never a truly straightforward platform. A platform that integrated with all the music platforms you love (Spotify, Apple Music, etc), where you could follow everyone you shared interests with, and where you could blog about your own interests.

Thus, Quality Notes was born.

Services we offer

Unlike other possible music rating platforms, Quality Notes is fully democratized, and fully simplified. The users decide the ratings, and the users decide which songs to rate. Moreover, because it's not just enough to simply rate the songs you like, Quality Notes provides the detailed notation tools required to endorse and promote your favorite songs so that the world can hear.

Our features:

  • A simple and responsive song rating system, so that your song reviewing experience is completely frictionless. Rate each song from 1 to 5 notes (because it's Quality "Notes"!)
  • The biggest, most robust database of songs any site has to offer. Quality Notes pulls from every possible resource so that you're informed of songs you'd otherwise never even hear of.
  • A ready-to-write blog write at your finger-tips, in case you want to step up your reviewing game and start providing written reviews, like the greats.
  • An internal and dynamic payment system for you to both charge as a creator and pay the creators on Quality Notes that you appreciate. Get paid for reviews, and financially encourage good content.
  • A social networking tool so that you can communicate and follow every single creator, reviewer, and user that you find interesting and compelling. Find your music people, and find the people that'll push the boundaries of your interests.
